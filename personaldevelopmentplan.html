Chapters 4 – 7 Summary and Reflection

Summary

  • Chapter 4 (“Achieve Your Goals”): Emphasizes on the necessity of setting clear written goals to give life direction.
  • Chapter 5 (“The Proper Use of Time”): Teaches that time is precious and must be used wisely for growth, not just activity.
  • Chapter 6 (“Surround Yourself with the Best People”): Reveals that the people you spend time with influence your outcomes.
  • Chapter 7 (“Be a Life Long Learner”): Encourages continuous learning as the foundation for long-term success.

Reflection

These chapters made me realize that success isn’t random, it’s built from daily habits and decisions. I have started to write my short and long-term goals in a planner and also avoid wasting time by prioritizing my tasks using a simple daily schedule. I also began spending more time with people who support my goals and challenge me to grow.
